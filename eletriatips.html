Welcome to the Tips page.

Here you can find a few helpful, early game tips you can use to make life easier.


Note: Until the server releases this will not have much content, and can change later due to changes with gameplay.


Mana

A new value has been added to the server. This will be available in the full release.

Mana will be really useful in a Mage type class. As such, getting mage based gear would be the best course of action for this style.

The more you have, the more magic attacks you can use.

Mana regens over-time, but slowly.


Combat

The World of Eletria uses a modified 1.8.9 style combat, relying heavily on high clicks/second for best results

For those without particularly high CPS may struggle with PvP combat. To balance this skill gap, strategies such as strafing around your target and jumping to hit criticals could make or break a PvP situation.

Another thing to close a gap between an opponent is to play more for better gear.
